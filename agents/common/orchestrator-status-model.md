# Minimum Orchestrator Status Model

```yaml
draft:
  owner: Product Owner
clarified:
  owner: Product Owner
designed:
  owner: Architect or Designer
planned:
  owner: Team Lead
in_progress:
  owner: Executor
under_review:
  owner: Reviewer
qa_check:
  owner: QA
accepted:
  owner: Product Owner or Team Lead
done:
  owner: system
blocked:
  owner: current assignee
```
